The Kp index reached 7 (G3 storm level) overnight following the arrival of a coronal mass ejection launched from the sun on 31 December.

Aurora was visible from Scotland and parts of northern England. Reports also came in from northern Europe and Canada.

Conditions are settling now, but remain elevated. Check the aurora forecast for tonight's outlook.
